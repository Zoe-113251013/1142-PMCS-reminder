W1 & W2 Orientation第一、二週 課程定位

Critical Alerts & Expectations重要選課須知與預期管理

  • English Proficiency B1+ Required英語能力要求 B1+ This is an EMI course. Weekly oral presentations and group discussions in English are mandatory. You must speak up every week. 這是一門全英授課 (EMI) 課程。每週皆需進行英語口頭報告與小組討論。您必須每週開口發言。
  • High Workload Commitment高強度的投入承諾 There are individual and group assignments every week. Please ensure you have sufficient time to commit (at least 3 hours/week outside class). 每週皆有個人作業與小組任務。請確認您有足夠的時間投入（課外每週至少3小時）。
  • Drop Policy退選建議機制 If you feel unprepared or overwhelmed by the requirements above, please drop the course early to allow waitlisted students to join. 若您覺得無法負荷或準備不及上述要求，請務必儘早退選，將名額讓給候補的同學。
Leave Policy (Applies Every Week)請假規定 (每週適用) If unable to attend, you MUST submit your leave request in advance on Moodle General ("Request for leave of absence & self-study note").若無法出席，請務必提前於 Moodle General 區塊提交請假單與自學筆記。
